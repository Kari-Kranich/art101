		Task 1:
		Make coffee
		1: Go to the kitchen with a Coffee Maker.
		2: Acquire a mug or a cup of your choice.
		3: Take a coffee bag of your choice.
		4: Put coffee grounds in the filter.
		5: Put the filler filter into the Coffee Maker.
		6: Put water in Coffee Maker.
		7: Start machine allowing it to brew.
		8: Take Coffee pot and pour coffee into the mug from step 2.
		9: Optional Step: Add Creamer of choice.

	

		Task 2:
		Computer Task: Tetris
		1. Display Board
		2. Create Piece at top of board
		3. Piece Fall down board at a certain speed
		4. Piece stops descending when it reaches the bottom of the board or lands on top of another piece
		5. If pieces line up in a row, delete the row, have pieces above descend till they reach the bottom of the board or land on top of another.
		6. If the piece lands at the top of the board, game over.
		7. If the piece does not land at the top of the board, loop back and generate a new piece.
